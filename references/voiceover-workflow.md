# Voiceover Workflow

Use this when generating口播, TTS audio, or audio direction for Qianchuan creative.

## Separate Spoken Script From Subtitles

Spoken script should sound human:

- Short clauses
- Natural hesitation where useful
- Concrete object words
- Avoid written-language transitions
- Avoid stacked adjectives
- Avoid reading product spec sheets aloud

Subtitles should be shorter:

- One idea per line
- More direct than the spoken script
- Highlight hook, pain, selling point, CTA

## Timed Voiceover Targets

Approximate Mandarin pace:

- Natural真人口播: 4-5 Chinese characters per second
- Fast ad口播: 5-6 Chinese characters per second
- TTS test voice: 4-5 Chinese characters per second for clarity

For 30 seconds, target 120-150 Chinese characters if no pauses are needed. If the video needs product-card hold time, target 105-130 characters.

## More Human口播 Rules

Prefer:

```text
我建议你先看这里
这个地方别忽略
很多人买错，其实不是因为价格
它不是单纯做软
贴脸用的东西，材质我会更在意
```

Avoid:

```text
本产品采用先进设计
有效解决用户痛点
高端品质值得拥有
强烈推荐大家购买
```

## Persona Variants

Generate口播 by persona when useful:

- Buyer/friend: natural, experiential, low pressure
- Host/主播: clearer CTA, faster rhythm
- Product manager/boss: explains design logic
- Evaluator: compares criteria, avoids exaggerated praise
- Consultant: only use if credentials are verified

## TTS And Audio

For test automation:

- Use system TTS only to validate the pipeline.
- Mark the voice as non-final if it sounds robotic.
- Keep spoken text shorter than video duration and leave 1-3 seconds for product-card hold.

For production:

- Prefer human recording or a commercial Chinese TTS with controllable emotion, speed, and timbre.
- Export clean voiceover separately from background music.
- Keep background music low enough that the voice remains clear.
- Add light room tone or soft BGM; completely dry voice often feels unfinished.

## Background Music And Sound

Add audio layers intentionally:

```text
voiceover: primary
BGM: low volume, no strong vocal
SFX: optional for hook, product reveal, coupon card
```

Rules:

- Do not let BGM compete with口播.
- Use softer music for premium/high-consumption positioning.
- Use light transitions only; avoid noisy meme effects unless the creative angle calls for it.
- If using stock music, confirm licensing before launch.
